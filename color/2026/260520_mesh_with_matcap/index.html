Mesh with Matcap Material Using Three.js

3D design and render: NMoroney

The matcap used was 736655_D9D8D5_2F281F_B1AEAB-512px from the repo nidorx/matcaps.

The object was a custom minimal quad poly designed in Blender. The PLY mesh was a triangulated export with MeshLab isotropic remeshing (1% target length and 15 degree crease angle).

The animation loop has separate rotation rates for x, y and z.

So it just spins and spins.

At one point I was stuck and found this video helpful (thanks Robot Bobby).
