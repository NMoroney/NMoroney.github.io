Color Terms and Stable Diffusion

The PDF of the CIC 2024 paper "Color Terms and Stable Diffusion" is available here.

From the abstract:

"This paper considers the following questions: how effectively are the colored objects generated? how do the colors generated by stable diffusion compare to human color naming? How might color terms be useful in visualizing properties and features of generative algorithms?"
