Unconstrained Web-Based Color Naming Experiment

At Electronic Imaging in 2003 in San Jose, I presented the following paper titled :

Unconstrained Web-Based Color Naming Experiment

The experiment had an optional comments box.

I read all of the comments as the data was submitted by participants.

One of these comments was :

"Wasn't sure if you wanted accurate or poetic names." Anonymous

Such a succinct observation about color naming.

Three years later, the term crowdsourcing was coined.
